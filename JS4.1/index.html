Homework guidelines

Late Days

Homework turned in on-time bonus. Homework can be turned in up to 48 hours past the deadline without penalty

Show Details
  • Homework will not be accepted past the late cutoff. You will receive a 0 for the assignment if is not turned by th late cutoff.
  • If you are not sure whether you can finish assignment by the late cutoff, you should turn it in before the deadline anyway to get partial credit. You are allowed to update your Github homework submission as many times as you need to before the late cutoff.
